20 /*
21  * Direct Face is a face implementation for single-thread program where
22  * application and forwarder are in the same thread
23  *
24  * In normal case, the logic of NDN face between app and forwarder is
25  * +------+ +--------+
26  * |app {face} <---> {face} fwd |
27  * +------+ +--------+
28  *
29  * With direct face, the logic is greatly simplified in single-thread scenario
30  * +------+ +--------+
31  * | app {direct face} fwd |
32  * +------+ +--------+
33  *
34  * In other words, direct face is an app face and a forwarder face, thus direct
35  * face provides APIS for both sides:
36  * APIs for app:
37  * * direct_face_express_interest
38  * * direct_face_register_prefix
39  * APIs for forwarder:
40  * * direct_face_send
41  * * direct_face_receive
42  */
